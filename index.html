About me

My name Ahmad Ikmal Bin Kasim and I live in Shah Alam. I've studied in SAMT Sultan Hisamudin for 3 months and completed my high school years at MRSM Langkawi at 2019. I have graduated from MRSM Langkawi with Principal's Special Award and first-class graduate. I've also managed to get 4A* and 2A in IGCSE 2018, 9A 1B in PT3 2017, Band 6 for Math and English while Band 5.1 for Science in Checkpoint IGCSE 2016.
